Financial KPIs
•	Day-zero revenue (first-dollar revenue from Tier 3 forensic reports)
•	Revenue run rate from Tier 3 NDA engagements (speed-to-revenue / cash-in-the-door)
•	Time-to-close / sales cycle length for Tier 3 contracts (market adoption velocity)
•	Cost savings to clients (developer hours saved vs. traditional audits)
•	False-positive reduction (efficiency metric tied to cost savings)
•	Total value at risk (TVL) protection / expected loss reduction from avoiding exploits
•	Liability and insurance impact (reduced insurance premiums or improved underwriter terms due to on-chain attestation)
•	Commercial deliverables / recurring monitoring revenue (continuous monitoring vs. one-off audit fees)
•	Market conversion from proposals to paid engagements (sales conversion rate for external collateral)
