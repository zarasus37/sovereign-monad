## What is Hepar?

Hepar is an AI-native forensic intelligence platform that continuously scans, analyzes, and monitors DeFi protocols and centralized exchanges in real time.  

Named after the biological liver, Hepar acts as a next-generation risk filtration system — identifying threats, validating opportunities, and delivering actionable intelligence before risks materialize.
