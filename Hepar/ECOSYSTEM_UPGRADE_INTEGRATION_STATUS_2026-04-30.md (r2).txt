## Doctrinal anchor

The first organ set is governed by **Biological Fidelity = Operational Standard** (`SIX_ORGAN_INSTITUTIONAL_DEPTH_BLUEPRINT.md` §0, `HEPAR_v2.0_SPECIFICATION.md` §0). Each organ — Hepar, Cortex, Synapse, Cardia, Pneuma, Vox — is named after a biological function and is required to perform that function at the standard implied by the name, not at the standard implied by the metaphor. This anchor is the architectural counterpart of MOF §17.8 (Hollow Convergence): a name claim without function clearance is not a depth implementation.

Every accepted upgrade track below is governed by the **Tier Discipline** ladder (`SIX_ORGAN_INSTITUTIONAL_DEPTH_BLUEPRINT.md` §3 and `HEPAR_v2.0_SPECIFICATION.md` §16): Advisory → Decision-Support → Authoritative. No upgrade track is declared past Advisory tier without the evidence required at the next tier.
