## Interpretation Boundary (binding)

These uplifts are from deterministic benchmark fixtures in `organ-runtime/src/tools/calibrate-organ-intelligence.ts`. They are valid for architecture and policy direction, but they are **not** a substitute for live-outcome calibration on production telemetry, and they are **not** evidence of biological-fidelity clearance under `SIX_ORGAN_INSTITUTIONAL_DEPTH_BLUEPRINT.md` §0.

Fixtures define the test, then the policy fits the fixture. A `1.00` accuracy on a deterministic fixture is **not** a `1.00` accuracy claim against live distribution. Treating fixture accuracy as live evidence would be an MOF §17.5 (False Validation) flag and a §17.8 (Hollow Convergence) flag.
